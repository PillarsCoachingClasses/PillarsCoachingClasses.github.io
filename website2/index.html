About Us

Pillars Coaching Classes Alipurduar is a growing educational institution in India that provides comprehensive test preparatory services to students preparing for class VI to X for C.B.S.E. as well as I.C.S.E. students.The first centre under Pillars was started in 2019 , offering coaching services for needy students.
